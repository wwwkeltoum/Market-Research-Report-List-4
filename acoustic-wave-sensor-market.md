<p><p>Acoustic Wave Sensors are divided into two main types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Sensors. SAW Sensors operate on the surface of a material, while BAW Sensors penetrate into the bulk of the material. SAW Sensors are known for their sensitivity and low cost, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, BAW Sensors offer high-frequency operation and high quality factor, making them ideal for precise measurements in harsh environments. Both types of sensors have unique characteristics and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
