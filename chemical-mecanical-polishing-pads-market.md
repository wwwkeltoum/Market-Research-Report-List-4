<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are used in the semiconductor industry for planarizing and smoothing surfaces during fabrication processes. There are two main types of CMP pads: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. </p><p>Hard CMP Pads are made of materials with a higher durometer, providing more rigidity and durability for aggressive material removal. Soft CMP Pads, on the other hand, have a lower durometer and are used for gentle surface finishing and polishing. Each type of pad is chosen based on the specific requirements of the CMP process.</p></p>
