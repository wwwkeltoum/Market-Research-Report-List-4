<p><p>Airborne Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) System market includes Topographic LiDAR and Bathymetric LiDAR. Topographic LiDAR is used for mapping land features and terrain, such as forests, rivers, and buildings, by measuring the elevation of the Earth's surface. Bathymetric LiDAR, on the other hand, is specifically designed to map underwater features, such as ocean floors and riverbeds. Both types of LiDAR systems are essential for various industries including urban planning, coastal management, infrastructure development, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
