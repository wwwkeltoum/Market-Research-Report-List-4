<p><p>Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is a wireless communication technology designed for short-range connectivity, focusing on energy efficiency. It enables devices like wearables, smart home products, and sensors to transmit small amounts of data with minimal power consumption, making it ideal for battery-operated devices. BLE operates in the 2.4 GHz ISM band and supports a variety of applications, ranging from health monitoring to smart agriculture.</p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of IoT devices and the rising demand for smart technologies across various sectors. The integration of BLE in consumer electronics, healthcare, and industrial applications underlines its versatility.
