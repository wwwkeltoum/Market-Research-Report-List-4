<p><strong>The Automotive Aluminum Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Type</li><li>Cold Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive aluminum die casting market includes two types: hot chamber type and cold chamber type. Hot chamber die casting is suitable for smaller parts with lower melting points, where the metal is melted in the casting machine itself. Cold chamber die casting, on the other hand, is used for larger parts with higher melting points, where the metal is melted in a separate furnace before being transferred to the casting machine. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
