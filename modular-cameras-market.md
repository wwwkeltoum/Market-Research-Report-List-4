<p><strong>The Modular Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modular cameras are available in two main types: CCD (charge-coupled device) and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor). CCD cameras provide high-quality images with low noise and better sensitivity to light, making them ideal for applications requiring high image quality, such as medical imaging or scientific research. On the other hand, CMOS cameras are more cost-effective, consume less power, and offer faster image processing speeds, making them suitable for applications where real-time image capture is crucial, such as surveillance or automotive cameras.</p></p>
