<p><p>The moulding starch market consists of three main types based on digestibility. Rapidly digestible starch is easily broken down and absorbed quickly by the body, providing immediate energy. Slowly digestible starch is processed at a slower rate, offering more sustained energy and stability in blood sugar levels. Resistant starch is not fully digested in the small intestine, promoting gut health and supporting weight management by enhancing satiety. These starch types cater to diverse consumer needs and applications in food and non-food industries.</p></p>
