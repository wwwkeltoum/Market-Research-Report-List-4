<p><p>Sliding miter saws are available in two main market types: single bevel and double bevel. Single bevel saws allow the blade to tilt to one side, typically to the left, making it easier to make bevel cuts in one direction. On the other hand, double bevel saws allow the blade to tilt to both the left and right sides, providing more versatility for making bevel cuts in multiple directions without having to flip the workpiece. Both types cater to different user preferences and cutting needs.</p></p>
