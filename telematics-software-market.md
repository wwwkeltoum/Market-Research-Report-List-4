<p><p>Telematics software market consists of three main types: GPS, Cellular, and Others. GPS telematics systems utilize satellite technology to accurately track the location and movement of vehicles. Cellular telematics systems use cellular networks to transmit data and information between vehicles and a central server. The "Others" category includes various types of telematics systems that may use different technologies such as RFID or Bluetooth for communication. Each type of telematics system offers unique features and benefits for businesses looking to optimize their fleet management and vehicle tracking.</p></p>
