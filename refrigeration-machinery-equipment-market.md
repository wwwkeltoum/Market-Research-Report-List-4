<p><p>Refrigeration machinery and equipment market can be categorized into three main types: compression, absorption, and steam jet refrigeration machines. Compression refrigeration machines work by compressing refrigerant gas to cool the air. Absorption refrigeration machines use a heat source to produce a refrigerant gas. Steam jet refrigeration machines utilize steam to create a vacuum that cools the air. Each type of refrigeration machine has its own advantages and applications in different industries such as food processing, cold storage, and air conditioning.</p></p>
