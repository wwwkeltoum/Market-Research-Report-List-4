<p><p>AC Linear Variable Displacement Transformers (LVDT) market types can be categorized into Free Core, Guided Core, Sprung Core, and Others. In Free Core LVDT, the core moves freely without constraint. In Guided Core LVDT, the core is guided by a bearing to ensure precise linear movement. Sprung Core LVDT uses a spring mechanism to provide stability and control over the core movement. Other types may include variations or combinations of these core designs to meet specific application requirements in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
