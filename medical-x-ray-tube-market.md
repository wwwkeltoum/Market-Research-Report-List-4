<p><strong>The Medical X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary Anode Medical X-Ray Tube and Rotating Anode Medical X-Ray Tube are two types of X-ray tubes used in medical imaging. Stationary Anode tubes have a fixed anode target, which limits their ability to handle high heat levels but are suitable for lower-power applications. Rotating Anode tubes have a rotating anode target, allowing them to handle higher heat levels and produce higher-quality images. Rotating Anode tubes are more commonly used in advanced medical imaging techniques such as CT scans and fluoroscopy.</p></p>
