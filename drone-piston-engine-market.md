<p><p>There are two main types of Drone Piston Engine markets: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines require only two movements – up and down – to complete a power cycle, making them lighter and simpler in design. They are commonly used in smaller drones due to their lighter weight. On the other hand, 4-stroke engines require four movements – intake, compression, power, and exhaust – to complete a power cycle, making them more fuel-efficient and powerful. They are often used in larger drones for increased performance.</p></p>
