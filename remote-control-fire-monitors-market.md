<p><ul><li>Electronic Monitor</li><li>Hydraulic Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Remote control fire monitors are used in firefighting operations to deliver water or foam from a safe distance. There are two main types of remote control fire monitors: electronic monitors and hydraulic monitors. Electronic monitors are controlled using electrical signals and are often more precise and easier to operate. Hydraulic monitors, on the other hand, are controlled using hydraulic systems and are typically more powerful and capable of delivering higher volumes of extinguishing agents. Both types play a crucial role in fire suppression efforts.</p></p>
