<p><strong>The Post-Reflow Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post-reflow machine market types include convection ovens and vapour phase ovens. Convection ovens use a fan to circulate hot air around the PCB, ensuring even heating and avoiding overheating. Vapour phase ovens use a special liquid that vaporizes and condenses on the PCB, providing uniform and controlled heating. Both types are used in the electronics industry for reflow soldering processes to ensure proper solder joints and component connections. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
