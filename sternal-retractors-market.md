<p><p>Sternal retractors are used in cardiac surgery to hold back the chest wall during the procedure. There are two main types of sternal retractors: sliding type and rotating type. Sliding type retractors allow for horizontal movement for easier access to the surgical area, while rotating type retractors provide the flexibility of rotating the blades for better visibility. Both types are essential tools for surgeons performing sternotomies and other cardiac procedures, ensuring a clear view of the operating field and optimal surgical outcomes.</p></p>
