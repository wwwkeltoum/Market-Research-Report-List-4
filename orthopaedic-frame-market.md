<p><p>Orthopaedic frame market is divided into three main types: Lower Extremity Frame, Upper Extremity Frame, and Others. Lower extremity frames are designed to help support and stabilize the lower body, particularly for patients with fractures or injuries in the legs or feet. Upper extremity frames are used to immobilize and provide support for the arms and hands. The "Others" category includes specialized frames for different parts of the body such as the spine or pelvis, catering to specific orthopaedic needs.</p></p>
