<p><p>The RF attenuators market is primarily categorized into fixed and variable attenuators. In contrast, variable attenuators allow users to adjust the attenuation level, offering flexibility for different scenarios and measurements. Both types are essential in managing signal levels in telecommunications, broadcasting, and electronic testing, ensuring optimal performance and preventing signal distortion or damage.</p></p>
