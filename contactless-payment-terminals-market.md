<p><p>Contactless payment terminals utilize various technologies such as Infrared, Near field communication (NFC), Bluetooth, Radio-frequency identification (RFID), and carrier-based mobile systems. Infrared technology enables data transfer through infrared rays, while NFC allows communication between devices in close proximity. Bluetooth technology enables wireless data transfer over short distances. RFID technology uses radio waves for communication. Carrier-based mobile systems utilize mobile networks for payment processing. Each of these technologies offers a different approach to facilitating secure and convenient contactless payments.</p></p>
