<p><p>Car interior leather market consists of two main types: Genuine Leather and Synthetic Leather. Genuine Leather is sourced from animal hides and is known for its high quality, durability, and luxurious feel. It is often more expensive than synthetic leather due to its natural origins. Synthetic Leather, on the other hand, is man-made and is typically made from materials like PU or PVC. It is generally more affordable and can mimic the look and feel of genuine leather without the use of animal products.</p></p>
