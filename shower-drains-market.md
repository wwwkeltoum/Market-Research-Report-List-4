<p><strong>The Shower Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Drains</li><li>Linear Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shower drains market encompasses two primary types: point drains and linear drains. Point drains, typically circular, are installed in the center of a shower floor, effectively collecting water from all directions. They are common in traditional designs. Linear drains, on the other hand, feature a long, narrow design, allowing for a more contemporary look while facilitating water flow along the entire length of the drain. Both types cater to diverse aesthetic preferences and functional needs in bathroom design.</p></p>
