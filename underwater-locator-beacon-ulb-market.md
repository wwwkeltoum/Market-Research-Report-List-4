<p><p>Underwater Locator Beacons (ULBs) are used to locate and recover aircraft flight data recorders and cockpit voice recorders in the event of a crash at sea. There are two main types of ULBs on the market: the 90-Day Underwater Locator Beacon operating at 37.5 kHz, and the Low-Frequency Underwater Locator Beacon operating at 8.8 kHz. The 90-Day ULB has a longer battery life and is designed for deeper water searches, while the Low-Frequency ULB is better suited for shallow water recoveries.</p></p>
