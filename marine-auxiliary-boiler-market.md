<p><strong>The Marine Auxiliary Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Tube Type</li><li>Smoke Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine auxiliary boilers can be classified into two main types: Water Tube Type and Smoke Tube Type. Water Tube Type boilers use large tubes filled with water while Smoke Tube Type boilers have smaller tubes filled with hot gases from the combustion process. Water Tube Type boilers are known for their efficiency and ability to handle higher pressures, making them ideal for larger marine vessels. Smoke Tube Type boilers are simpler in design and easier to maintain, making them suitable for smaller vessels with lower power requirements.</p></p>
