<p><ul><li>CBTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>ATC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway signaling market can be classified into three main types - CBTC (Communication Based Train Control), PTC (Positive Train Control), and ATC (Automatic Train Control). CBTC utilizes communication technology for precise train control and allows for closer train spacing, increasing capacity and efficiency. PTC is designed to prevent train collisions and derailments by automatically stopping trains in unsafe conditions. ATC is used for automated train operations, regulating train movements and ensuring safe and efficient transportation for passengers.</p></p>
