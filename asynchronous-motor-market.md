<p><p>Asynchronous motors are commonly divided into two main markets: single-phase asynchronous motors and three-phase asynchronous motors. Single-phase asynchronous motors are typically smaller in size and used in smaller appliances and power tools, while three-phase asynchronous motors are larger and used in industrial machinery and equipment. Single-phase motors are simpler in design and less expensive, while three-phase motors offer higher efficiency and power output. Both types of motors operate without the need for external synchronization with the power supply.</p></p>
