<p><p>The dog harness market features three primary types: Back-Clip Harnesses, Front-Clip Harnesses, and Dual-Clip Harnesses. Back-Clip Harnesses attach at the dog's back, promoting comfortable movement, ideal for relaxed walks. Front-Clip Harnesses attach at the front, providing better control and discouraging pulling, making them suitable for training. Dual-Clip Harnesses offer versatility with attachment points on both the front and back, allowing dog owners to choose the method that best fits their needs and the dog's behavior.</p></p>
