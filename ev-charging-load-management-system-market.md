<p><p>Dynamic management in the EV charging load management system involves real-time adjustments to charging rates based on grid demand, user preferences, and energy prices. It allows for flexible and optimized charging schedules to minimize strain on the grid. </p><p>Static management, on the other hand, involves pre-set schedules or limits for charging rates, without the ability to adapt to changing conditions. While it is simpler to implement, it may not be as efficient in optimizing grid resources and user convenience.</p></p>
