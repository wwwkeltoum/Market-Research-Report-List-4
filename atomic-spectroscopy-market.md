<p><p>The atomic spectroscopy market includes various techniques for elemental analysis. X-ray Fluorescence Spectroscopy enables qualitative and quantitative chemical analysis by measuring X-ray emissions. X-ray Diffraction Spectroscopy assesses crystalline structures through diffraction patterns. Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectroscopy (ICP-MS) offers high sensitivity for trace element detection. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) Spectroscopy allows for the measurement of metal concentrations. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy quantifies elements by observing absorption of light. Elemental Analyzers analyze sample compositions for specific elements efficiently.</p></p>
