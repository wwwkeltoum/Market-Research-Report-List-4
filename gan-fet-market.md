<p><strong>The GaN FET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN FET Market includes two main types: Depletion Mode and Enhancement Mode. Depletion Mode GaN FETs are normally ON devices, meaning they conduct current when no voltage is applied. They are suitable for high-power applications. Enhancement Mode GaN FETs are normally OFF devices, requiring a specific voltage to turn them ON. They are ideal for low-power applications. Both types offer high efficiency, fast switching speeds, and high-frequency operation, making them popular choices in industries such as power supplies, electric vehicles, and RF amplifiers.</p></p>
