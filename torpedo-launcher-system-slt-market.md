Underwater Tubes are integrated into the hull of a submarine or ship, allowing for the launch of torpedoes while submerged. Deck-mounted Units are installed on the deck of a ship, enabling the firing of torpedoes while on the surface. Both systems play a crucial role in naval defense by providing the capability to launch torpedoes against enemy targets from both underwater and surface platforms.</p></p>
