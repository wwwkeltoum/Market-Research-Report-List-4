Fixed HSMs are mounted in a specific location and provide continuous monitoring of heat stress conditions in that area. Portable HSMs, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily moved around to measure heat stress levels in different locations. They are often used by athletes, outdoor workers, and emergency responders to monitor their exposure to heat stress in real-time.</p></p>
