<p><p>Clinical nutrition market includes three main types: oral nutrition, enteral nutrition, and parenteral nutrition. Oral nutrition involves consuming nutrients through food or supplements. Enteral nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly into the gastrointestinal tract through a tube. Parenteral nutrition involves delivering nutrients intravenously when the digestive system is unable to absorb them. Each type of nutrition market caters to different needs and conditions of patients, providing necessary nutrients for optimal health and wellness.</p></p>
