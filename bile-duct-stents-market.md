<p><strong>The Bile Duct Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Covered Type</li><li>Uncovered Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bile duct stents market is categorized into covered and uncovered types. Covered stents have a synthetic material that encases the mesh, offering enhanced biocompatibility and reduced tissue ingrowth, making them ideal for treating malignant strictures. Uncovered stents, on the other hand, consist solely of a bare metal framework, allowing for better drainage in non-malignant conditions but may have higher rates of re-obstruction due to tissue growth. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on patient conditions and preferences.</p></p>
