<p><p>The direct semiconductor laser market can be categorized into two main types based on power output: high power and low power. High power semiconductor lasers are designed for applications that require intense beams of light, such as industrial cutting and welding. These lasers typically have output powers ranging from a few watts to several kilowatts. On the other hand, low power semiconductor lasers are used in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and medical devices, with output powers typically ranging from a few milliwatts to a few watts.</p></p>
