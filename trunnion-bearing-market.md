Integral Bearings are integrated into the trunnion structure for compactness and simplicity. Other types of trunnion bearings include custom or hybrid designs tailored to specific application requirements. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on factors such as load capacity, speed, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
