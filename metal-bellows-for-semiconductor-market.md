<p><strong>The Metal Bellows for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Welded Metal Bellows</li><li>Electroformed Metal Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal bellows for semiconductor market includes edge welded metal bellows and electroformed metal bellows. Edge welded metal bellows are manufactured by welding thin metal diaphragms together at the edges to form a bellows structure. Electroformed metal bellows are produced through an electroforming process where a mandrel is coated with metal through electrolysis to create the bellows shape. Both types of metal bellows are used in semiconductor applications for their high precision, flexibility, and resistance to harsh operating conditions.</p></p>
