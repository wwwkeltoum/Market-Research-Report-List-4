<p><p>Enterprise Resource Planning System (ERP) solutions are available in two main market types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. Cloud-based ERP, on the other hand, is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet. Cloud-based ERP offers more flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to on-premise ERP, making it a popular choice for many organizations.</p></p>
