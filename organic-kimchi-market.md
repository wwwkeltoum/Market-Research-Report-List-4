Kkakdugi consists of diced radish and offers a crunchy texture. Pa-kimchi uses scallions for a fragrant twist, while Oi Sobagi includes cucumbers, making it a light and crisp option. Each type caters to diverse consumer preferences within the organic segment.</p></p>
