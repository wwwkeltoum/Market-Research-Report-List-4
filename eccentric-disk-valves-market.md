<p><ul><li>Wafer Type</li><li>Lug Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are primarily classified into two types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are designed to fit between flanges in a pipeline without the need for additional flange holes. They are lightweight, compact, and easy to install. On the other hand, lug type valves have threaded inserts or bolts on either side of the valve body for easy installation and removal. They are commonly used in applications where the valve needs to be inline with the pipeline.</p></p>
