<p><p>Fish counting systems come in two main types: stationary and mobile. Stationary systems are typically installed at fixed locations such as dams or fishways to accurately count fish as they pass through. These systems are reliable and provide consistent data over time. Mobile systems, on the other hand, are portable and can be easily moved to different locations to count fish in multiple areas. While stationary systems are more commonly used, mobile systems offer flexibility and can cover a larger area.</p></p>
