<p><p>Automatic sliding doors come in two main types: bi-parting and single sliding doors. Bi-parting doors consist of two panels that open from the center, creating a wide opening for large crowds or materials to pass through. They are commonly used in commercial settings such as airports and shopping malls. Single sliding doors, on the other hand, have one panel that slides to the side to open. They are often used in smaller spaces like offices or residential buildings. Both types offer convenience, efficiency, and ease of access.</p></p>
