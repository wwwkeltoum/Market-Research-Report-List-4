<p><p>Maqui berry powder is commonly produced using two main methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves spraying a liquid form of the maqui berry extract into a hot air chamber, which quickly evaporates the liquid leaving behind a fine powder. Freeze drying, on the other hand, involves freezing the maqui berry extract and then slowly removing the ice crystals through sublimation, resulting in a powder with a higher nutrient retention. Both methods have their unique advantages and are popular choices in the market.</p></p>
