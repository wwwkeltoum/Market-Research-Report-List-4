<p><p>The smart meter market is typically categorized into two main types: single-phase smart meters and three-phase smart meters. Single-phase smart meters are designed for residential and small commercial applications, measuring energy consumption in homes and smaller businesses. They are simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, three-phase smart meters cater to larger commercial and industrial users, providing more accurate monitoring of higher electricity loads. These meters support complex energy management solutions and are essential for businesses with substantial power needs.</p></p>
