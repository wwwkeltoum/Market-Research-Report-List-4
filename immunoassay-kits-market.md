<p><p>Immunoassay kits are widely used in the healthcare industry for detecting target molecules in a sample. Fluorometric immunoassay kits utilize fluorescence to detect the presence of the target molecule, providing a high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, colorimetric immunoassay kits rely on changes in color to identify the target molecule, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types of kits offer a quick and efficient way to analyze samples, making them essential tools for various applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
