<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Film for Non-destructive Testing (NDT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li><li>Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray film for non-destructive testing (NDT) is categorized into two main types: non-screen type films and screen type films. Non-screen type films are sensitive to X-rays and require higher exposure times, making them ideal for applications needing detailed imaging, such as inspecting welds. In contrast, screen type films are used in conjunction with fluorescent screens, allowing for shorter exposure times and improved image quality. These films are essential for detecting flaws in materials without causing damage.</p></p>
