<p><strong>The InP Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LEC Grown InP</li><li>VGF Grown InP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>InP wafers are available in two main market types: LEC grown InP and VGF grown InP. LEC grown InP wafers are produced using liquid encapsulated Czochralski crystal growth method, resulting in high purity and low dislocation density wafers. On the other hand, VGF grown InP wafers are produced using the vertical gradient freeze method, offering cost-effective solutions with good crystal quality. Both types cater to different market needs and applications, providing options for high-performance or budget-sensitive projects.</p></p>
