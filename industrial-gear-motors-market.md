<p><p>Industrial gear motors market can be broadly categorized into gearbox and gear motors segments. Gearboxes are mechanical devices that transmit power from one component to another, providing speed and torque conversions in the process. Gear motors, on the other hand, combine a gear reducer with an electric motor to deliver high torque output at low speeds. Both gearbox and gear motors play a crucial role in various industrial applications, including conveyor systems, packaging machines, and robotics.</p></p>
