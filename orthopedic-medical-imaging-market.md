<p><p>Orthopedic medical imaging market consists of various types such as X-ray systems, CT scanners, ultrasound systems, and others. X-ray systems utilize electromagnetic radiation to create images of bones and joints, while CT scanners use computer-processed X-rays to produce detailed cross-sectional images. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to generate images of soft tissues and internal structures. Other imaging modalities may include MRI machines, PET scanners, and bone densitometers, all of which play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring orthopedic conditions.</p></p>
