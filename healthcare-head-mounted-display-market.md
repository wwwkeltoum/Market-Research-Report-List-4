<p><p>Healthcare Head Mounted Display market consists of two main types: Helmet Mounted Display and Eye Wear Display. Helmet Mounted Display is a type of head mounted display that is worn like a helmet and provides information directly in the wearer's field of view. It is commonly used in medical procedures and surgeries. Eye Wear Display, on the other hand, is a more lightweight and portable option that is worn like a pair of glasses. It is often used for diagnostic purposes and medical education. Both types offer hands-free access to important information, making them valuable tools in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
