<p><p>Near Infrared Spectrum Instruments come in three main types - Filter Type, Dispersion Type, and Fourier Transform Type. Filter Type instruments use filters to isolate specific wavelengths of light, Dispersion Type instruments use diffraction gratings to disperse light into different wavelengths, and Fourier Transform Type instruments use interferometers to measure the spectrum of light. Each type of instrument has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
