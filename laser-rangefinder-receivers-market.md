<p><p>The Laser Rangefinder Receivers market comprises three key types: InGaAs PIN detectors, InGaAs APD detectors, and others. InGaAs PIN detectors are known for their cost-effectiveness and efficiency in detecting near-infrared light, making them suitable for short to medium-range applications. InGaAs APD detectors offer higher sensitivity and are ideal for longer range detection, though at a higher cost. The "Others" category includes alternative technologies, such as Si photodiodes and Ge detectors, catering to specialized applications within the market.</p></p>
