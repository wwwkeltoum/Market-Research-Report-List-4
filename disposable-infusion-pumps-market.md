<p><p>Disposable infusion pumps market consists of two main types: continuous rate pumps and variable rate pumps. Continuous rate pumps deliver medication or fluids at a constant rate over a period of time, making them ideal for long-term treatments. Variable rate pumps, on the other hand, allow healthcare providers to adjust the flow rate based on the patient's specific needs, offering more flexibility in treatment delivery. Both types of pumps contribute to the growing demand for disposable infusion pumps in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
