<p><p>Diagnostic catheters are medical devices used in various procedures to diagnose and treat heart conditions. Angiography catheters are used to visualize blood vessels and diagnose blockages or abnormalities in the heart. On the other hand, electrophysiology (EP) catheters are specialized tools used to study and treat heart rhythm disorders. These catheters are inserted through blood vessels and guided to the heart, allowing healthcare professionals to safely and accurately diagnose and treat cardiovascular conditions. Both types of catheters play a critical role in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
