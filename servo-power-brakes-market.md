<p><p>Servo Power Brakes come in three main types: Vacuum Servo Brakes, Pneumatic Servo Brakes, and Hydraulic Servo Brakes. Vacuum Servo Brakes use vacuum pressure to assist in braking, while Pneumatic Servo Brakes utilize air pressure for braking assistance. Hydraulic Servo Brakes, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic fluid to aid in braking. Each type of Servo Power Brake offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications based on their design and operating principles.</p></p>
