<p><p>An Integrated Video Surveillance System market includes two main types: Network Video Recorder (NVR) System and Digital Video Recorder (DVR) System. NVR systems use IP cameras to record and store video footage on a network server, offering flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, DVR systems use analog cameras to record footage on a physical hard drive within the system. Both systems allow for remote viewing and playback of video footage, providing security and monitoring capabilities for various environments.</p></p>
