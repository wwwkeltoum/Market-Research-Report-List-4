<p><p>Hot packs are commonly available in disposable and reusable forms. Disposable hot packs are designed for one-time use and are typically activated by breaking a pouch inside the pack to create a chemical reaction that generates heat. Reusable hot packs, on the other hand, can be reheated multiple times by boiling or placing in the microwave. Reusable hot packs are often preferred for their environmental friendliness and cost-effectiveness in the long run, while disposable hot packs offer convenience and immediate use without the need for reheating.</p></p>
