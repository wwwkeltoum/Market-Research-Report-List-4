<p><p>Destemmer market offers two types: mobile destemmers and stationary destemmers. Mobile destemmers are designed for vineyards or wineries with limited space, as they can easily be moved around different areas for grape processing. On the other hand, stationary destemmers are ideal for larger operations with a fixed processing area. Stationary destemmers are typically more heavy-duty and efficient, making them suitable for high-volume grape processing. Both types of destemmers play a crucial role in the winemaking process by separating grapes from stems before crushing and fermentation.</p></p>
