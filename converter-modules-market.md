<p><ul><li>DC-DC Converter Module</li><li>DC-AC Converter Module</li><li>AC-DC Converter Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Converter modules are essential components in power electronics, facilitating energy conversion across various applications. The DC-DC converter module transforms direct current from one voltage level to another, making it ideal for battery-powered devices. The DC-AC converter module, also known as an inverter, converts direct current into alternating current, essential for powering AC devices. Meanwhile, the AC-DC converter module changes alternating current into direct current, commonly used in power supplies for electronic equipment. Together, these modules support efficient energy management in diverse sectors.</p></p>
