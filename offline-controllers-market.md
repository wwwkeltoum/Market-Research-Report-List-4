<p><ul><li>Current Mode</li><li>Voltage Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offline controllers market can be divided into two types - current mode and voltage mode. In current mode controllers, the output voltage is regulated by adjusting the duty cycle based on the sensed output current. On the other hand, voltage mode controllers regulate the output voltage by adjusting the duty cycle based on the sensed output voltage. Both types offer different advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
