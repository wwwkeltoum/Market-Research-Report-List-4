<p><p>The fiber optic preform market consists of various manufacturing processes, each producing high-quality glass preforms essential for fiber optic cables. Vapor Axial Deposition (VAD) offers high purity and excellent performance. Outside Vapour Deposition (OVD) creates preforms with optimal strength and reduced loss. Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition (PCVD) enables precise control over composition and refractive index. Meanwhile, Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition (MCVD) allows for the layering of materials, enhancing light transmission. Together, these methods cater to diverse application needs in telecommunications and data transfer.</p></p>
