<p><p>Tripod turnstiles are available in two main types in the market: semi-automatic and fully automatic. On the other hand, fully automatic turnstiles operate without the need for manual intervention, utilizing sensors and electronic controls to allow or restrict access. Both types of turnstiles offer security and control over pedestrian flow, with fully automatic turnstiles providing a more seamless and efficient experience.</p></p>
