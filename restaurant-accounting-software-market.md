<p><p>Restaurant accounting software market can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted and accessed online, offering flexibility and real-time updates. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and maintained on the restaurant's own servers, providing control over data security and customization. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of restaurant owners, with cloud-based solutions being more popular for their convenience and accessibility, while on-premises solutions are favored for their security and customizability.</p></p>
