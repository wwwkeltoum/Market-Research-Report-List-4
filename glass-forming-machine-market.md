<p><p>Glass forming machines are used in the production of glass containers. There are different types of glass forming machines in the market including Blow & Blow, Press & Blow, and Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB). </p><p>Blow & Blow machines are used for the production of wide neck containers. Press & Blow machines are used for the production of narrow neck containers. NNPB machines are a combination of both press and blow technologies and are used for the production of narrow neck containers with precision neck finishes. Each type of machine has its own unique advantages and is used depending on the specific requirements of the production process.</p></p>
