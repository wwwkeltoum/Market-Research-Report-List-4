<p><p>BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are categorized based on thickness, influencing their applications and properties. Films below 15 microns are primarily used for flexible packaging and labels, offering lightweight solutions. The 15-30 micron range is popular for food packaging, combining durability with clarity. Films between 30-45 microns provide enhanced strength for industrial uses, while those above 45 microns are suited for applications requiring high barrier properties, such as heavy-duty packaging and protective wraps. Each category targets specific market needs effectively.</p></p>
