<p><p>The automotive lighting market is categorized into halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lighting is the traditional option, offering brightness at a low cost but with shorter lifespan and higher energy consumption. HID lighting provides greater efficiency and brightness, making it popular in premium vehicles, though it requires more complex fixtures. LED lighting is gaining traction for its longevity, energy efficiency, and versatility in design, increasingly becoming the standard for modern vehicles.</p></p>
