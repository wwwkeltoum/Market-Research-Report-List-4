<p><p>Cough syrup markets can be classified into three main types: cough suppressants, expectorants, and antihistamines. Cough suppressants work by inhibiting the cough reflex in the brain, providing relief from a dry, persistent cough. Expectorants help thin and loosen mucus in the airways, making it easier to cough up. Antihistamines are used to reduce allergy symptoms that can cause a cough. Each type of cough syrup targets different aspects of cough symptoms to provide relief and improve respiratory health.</p></p>
