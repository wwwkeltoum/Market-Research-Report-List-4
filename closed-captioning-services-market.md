<p><p>Closed captioning services are available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise closed captioning involves installing and maintaining captioning software directly on the user's hardware system. This option provides more control and customization but requires regular updates and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based closed captioning services operate through an internet connection, allowing users to access captioning features remotely. This option offers convenience, scalability, and automatic updates but may have limited customization options compared to on-premise solutions.</p></p>
