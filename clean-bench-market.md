<p><strong>The Clean Bench Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Flow Clean Bench</li><li>Horizontal Flow Clean Bench</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clean benches are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, research labs, and electronics manufacturing. Vertical flow clean benches channel air flow from top to bottom, creating a sterile environment for sensitive materials. Horizontal flow clean benches, on the other hand, direct air flow from the back to the front, providing a clean workspace for activities like sample prep and testing. Both types are essential for maintaining cleanliness, hygiene, and product integrity in controlled environments.</p></p>
