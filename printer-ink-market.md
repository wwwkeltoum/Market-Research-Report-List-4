<p><ul><li>Dye Type</li><li>Pigment Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printer ink market is primarily divided into two types: dye-based and pigment-based inks. Dye-based inks dissolve in the liquid medium, providing vibrant colors and smooth gradients, making them ideal for photo printing, although they may fade over time. In contrast, pigment-based inks consist of solid particles suspended in liquid, offering superior longevity and water resistance, making them suitable for documents and archival purposes. Both types cater to different printing needs, influencing consumer and industrial demand.</p></p>
