<p><p>LVDS connectors can be categorized into two main types: one-piece and two-piece. On the other hand, two-piece LVDS connectors consist of two separate parts that need to be connected during assembly. One-piece connectors are typically easier to install and provide a more secure connection, while two-piece connectors offer more flexibility in terms of customization and repair options.
