Electric actuators utilize electric power for operation, hydraulic actuators use fluids for power transmission, and pneumatic actuators operate using compressed air. Each type of actuator offers unique benefits and capabilities, making them suitable for different applications based on factors such as precision, speed, and load capacity.</p></p>
