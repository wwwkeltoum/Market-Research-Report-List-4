<p><p>PM2.5 monitoring equipment comes in various types, including TEOM monitors, Beta Attenuation Monitors, and others. TEOM monitors use a filter to measure the mass of particles collected, providing accurate real-time data. Beta Attenuation Monitors measure particle concentrations by detecting the decrease in beta radiation as particles collect on a filter. Other types of monitors may use different technologies such as laser-based systems or optical particle counters to measure PM2.5 levels in the air.</p></p>
