<p><p>Silicone molding compounds are used in two main types of molding processes: Press Molding and Injection Molding. Press Molding involves placing the silicone compound into a mold and applying pressure to shape it into the desired form. Injection Molding, on the other hand, involves injecting the silicone compound into a mold at high pressure to create intricate shapes and details. Both processes are commonly used in the production of a wide range of silicone products for various industries.</p></p>
