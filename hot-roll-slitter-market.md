<p><ul><li>Log Slitting</li><li>Rewind Slitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot roll slitter market offers two main types of slitting techniques: log slitting and rewind slitting. Log slitting involves cutting a large master roll into smaller, narrower rolls known as logs. This method is commonly used for high-volume productions. On the other hand, rewind slitting involves rewinding the material onto smaller cores after cutting. This method is preferred for materials that are sensitive to tension or require precise winding. Both techniques have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
