<p><p>Samplers market types include automatic, probe, vacuum, composite, and others. Automatic samplers are fully automated and operate without human intervention. Probe samplers use a device to collect samples from a specific depth or location. Vacuum samplers utilize suction to gather samples from a source. Composite samplers mix multiple samples together to create a representative composite sample. Other types of samplers include grab samplers, bailers, and passive samplers, each with unique methods of sample collection.</p></p>
