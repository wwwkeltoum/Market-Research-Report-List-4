<p><strong>The Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car wrap film market types include cast film and calendered film. Cast film is known for its high quality and durability, as it is manufactured by pouring liquid resin onto a casting sheet and allowing it to cure. This process results in a thin and flexible film that conforms well to curves and contours. Calendered film, on the other hand, is produced by passing the vinyl through a series of heated rollers, making it less expensive but also less conformable than cast film.</p></p>
