<p><p>Smart stethoscopes come in two main types: wireless stethoscopes and stethoscopes with wires. Wireless stethoscopes use Bluetooth technology to transmit sound to a connected device, offering freedom of movement and ease of use. Stethoscopes with wires, on the other hand, have a physical connection to the device, ensuring stable and reliable sound transmission. Both types offer advanced features such as noise cancellation, audio recording, and digital sound visualization, making them essential tools for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
