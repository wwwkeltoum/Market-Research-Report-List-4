<p><p>Petrochemical heaters are used in various applications such as cracking furnaces, steam superheaters, and hot oil heaters. Cracking furnaces are used for the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons to produce ethylene and other chemicals. Steam superheaters are used to increase the temperature of steam for enhanced efficiency in industrial processes. Hot oil heaters heat thermal fluids for processes such as heating, drying, and cooling. Each type of heater serves a specific function in the petrochemical industry, contributing to improved process efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
