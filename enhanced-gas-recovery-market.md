Carbon Dioxide Based EGR involves injecting carbon dioxide to improve oil recovery by reducing the oil viscosity and improving contact between oil and reservoir rock. Other types of EGR may include using different gases such as natural gas or flue gas. Overall, EGR techniques aim to maximize oil and gas extraction from reservoirs.</p></p>
