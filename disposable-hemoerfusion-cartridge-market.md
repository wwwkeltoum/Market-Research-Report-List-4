<p><strong>The Disposable Hemoerfusion Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Charcoal Hemoperfusion</li><li>Certain Resins Hemoperfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable hemoperfusion cartridge market primarily consists of two types: charcoal hemoperfusion and certain resin hemoperfusion. Charcoal hemoperfusion utilizes activated charcoal to adsorb toxins and metabolites from the bloodstream, making it effective for treating drug overdoses and poisonings. Certain resin hemoperfusion, on the other hand, employs specialized resins to selectively remove specific substances, such as bile acids and uremic toxins, enhancing treatment options for patients with renal failure or liver diseases. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes by facilitating toxin removal from circulation.</p></p>
