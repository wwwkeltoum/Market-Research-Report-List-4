<p><p>Polystyrene tubes are commonly used in laboratory settings for storage and transportation of samples. There are two main types of Polystyrene tubes based on their bottom shape: Round Bottom and Conical Bottom. Round Bottom tubes are ideal for mixing and centrifugation, as they allow for even distribution of samples. On the other hand, Conical Bottom tubes are preferred for easy sample retrieval and efficient pelleting of particles. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific needs of the laboratory application.</p></p>
