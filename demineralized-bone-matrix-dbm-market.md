DBM is available in several forms, including gel, putty, putty with chips, and other forms. The gel form is more fluid and easily adaptable for filling voids, while putty is moldable and cohesive. Putty with chips combines the benefits of putty with added structural support from bone chips. Other forms of DBM may include sponges or sheets for specific applications.</p></p>
