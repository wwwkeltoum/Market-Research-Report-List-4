<p><p>The area image sensor market is primarily segmented into three types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and others. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption and high-speed performance, making them popular in mobile devices and digital cameras. CCD sensors offer superior image quality and sensitivity, often used in professional photography and scientific applications. Other types may include specialized sensors like hybrid or global shutter technologies, catering to niche applications in industrial and medical imaging markets.</p></p>
