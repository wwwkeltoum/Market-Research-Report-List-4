<p><p>Forklift extensions can be classified into two main types: roller and box market. Roller forklift extensions consist of a series of rollers that enable smooth movement of goods onto the forks of the forklift. They are ideal for transporting items such as pipes, barrels, and heavy cylindrical loads. On the other hand, box market forklift extensions feature a flat surface that provides additional support for carrying large and awkwardly-shaped loads, such as pallets, crates, and boxes. Both types of extensions enhance the versatility and efficiency of forklift operations.</p></p>
