<p><p>Effervescent Tablet Manufacturing Services Market Types include Wet Granulation, Dry Granulation, and Direct Compression. Wet Granulation involves mixing active pharmaceutical ingredients with excipients and a liquid binder, followed by drying and granulating the mixture. Dry Granulation involves compressing the powder mixture without the use of liquid binders. Direct Compression is a simpler method where all ingredients are directly compressed into tablets without the need for granulation. Each method offers different benefits such as cost-effectiveness, process efficiency, and product stability.</p></p>
