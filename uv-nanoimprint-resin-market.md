<p><p>UV Nanoimprint Resin is used in two main types of applications in the market - Permanent Layer and Sacrificial Layer. The Permanent Layer is a stable and long-lasting layer that is imprinted onto a substrate and remains in place permanently. On the other hand, the Sacrificial Layer is a temporary layer that can be easily removed after imprinting, leaving behind a clean surface. Both types of UV Nanoimprint Resin offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
