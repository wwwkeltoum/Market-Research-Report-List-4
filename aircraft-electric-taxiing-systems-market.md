<p><p>Aircraft Electric Taxiing Systems market includes two types: Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS and Vehicular Aircraft EGTS. Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS are electric taxiing systems that are installed on aircrafts without their own power source, such as towbar-less pushback vehicles. Vehicular Aircraft EGTS are electric taxiing systems that are integrated into the aircraft itself, allowing it to taxi without the use of its main engines. Both types of systems aim to reduce fuel consumption, emissions, and noise levels during ground operations, making them more efficient and environmentally friendly.</p></p>
