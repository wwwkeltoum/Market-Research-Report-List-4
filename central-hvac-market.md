<p><strong>The Central HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split System Central HVAC</li><li>Packaged Unit Central HVAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central HVAC systems can be categorized into two main types: split system and packaged unit. Split system central HVAC consists of two separate units, one indoor and one outdoor, connected by refrigerant lines. This type is commonly used in residential buildings. Packaged unit central HVAC, on the other hand, combines all components in a single unit located either on the roof or next to the building. This type is often found in commercial buildings or larger residential properties. Both systems work to efficiently heat and cool indoor spaces.</p></p>
