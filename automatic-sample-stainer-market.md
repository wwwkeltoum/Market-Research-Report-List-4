<p><ul><li>Continuous Feed Stainer</li><li>Batch Stainer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic sample stainers come in two main market types: continuous feed stainers and batch stainers. Continuous feed stainers are designed for high-throughput laboratories that require a constant and uninterrupted flow of samples. They automatically process samples as they are fed into the machine, allowing for efficient and continuous operation. On the other hand, batch stainers are ideal for smaller labs or those with varying sample volumes. They process samples in batches, providing flexibility and the ability to handle different numbers of samples at a time.</p></p>
