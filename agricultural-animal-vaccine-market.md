<p><p>The Agricultural Animal Vaccine Market includes three main types of vaccines: Live Attenuated Vaccines, Inactivated Vaccines, and Others. Live attenuated vaccines contain weakened forms of the virus or bacteria, stimulating a strong immune response without causing disease. Inactivated vaccines use killed pathogens to trigger an immune response. Other types of vaccines include subunit vaccines, DNA vaccines, and recombinant vaccines which utilize specific parts of the pathogen to induce immunity. Each type of vaccine has its own advantages and limitations in protecting agricultural animals against diseases.</p></p>
