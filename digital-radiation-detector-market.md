<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Radiation Detector market consists of three main types: Gas Ionization Detectors, Semiconductor Detectors, and Scintillation Detectors. Gas Ionization Detectors measure radiation by ionizing gases within a chamber. Semiconductor Detectors use a semiconductor material to detect radiation and convert it into an electrical signal. Scintillation Detectors use crystals that emit light when radiation interacts with them, which is then converted into an electrical signal. Each type of detector has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different applications in the radiation detection industry.</p></p>
