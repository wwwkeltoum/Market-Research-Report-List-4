<p><p>Portable Power Sprayers come in two main types - Direct Coupling Type and Belt Driven Type. Direct Coupling Type sprayers have the engine directly connected to the pump, offering higher efficiency and power transfer. On the other hand, Belt Driven Type sprayers use a belt to connect the engine and pump, providing quieter operation and smoother performance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in the market, with each offering unique advantages in terms of efficiency and performance.</p></p>
