<p><p>Electric scooter tires market includes two main types: tubeless tires and tubed tires. Tubeless tires are becoming increasingly popular due to their lower maintenance requirements and reduced risk of punctures. They have a special lining inside the tire that prevents air from escaping, making them less prone to flats. On the other hand, tubed tires require inner tubes to hold air and are typically easier to repair in case of a puncture. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different preferences in the electric scooter industry.</p></p>
