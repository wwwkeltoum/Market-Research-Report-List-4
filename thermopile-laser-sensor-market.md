<p><strong>The Thermopile Laser Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volume Absorber Sensors</li><li>Surface Absorber Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermopile laser sensor market is categorized into two main types: volume absorber sensors and surface absorber sensors. Volume absorber sensors detect laser energy through the absorption of light within a bulk material, making them suitable for high-energy laser applications. In contrast, surface absorber sensors capture laser energy at the surface of the sensor, often providing faster response times and enhanced sensitivity. Both types are essential for precise temperature measurement and thermal sensing in various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
