<p><p>Composite structural repairs market types include hand lay-up, vacuum infusion, autoclave, and others. Hand lay-up involves manually applying resin and fiberglass layers to repair the composite structure. Vacuum infusion uses a vacuum to distribute resin evenly throughout the repair area. Autoclave repair involves placing the damaged part in an autoclave for curing under heat and pressure. Other methods may include filament winding or resin transfer molding. Each method has its advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the repair project.</p></p>
