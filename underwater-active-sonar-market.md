<p><ul><li>Multi-Beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater active sonar market is segmented into two main types: Multi-Beam Sonar System and Side Scan Sonar System. Multi-Beam Sonar System uses multiple beams of sound waves to provide a detailed image of the underwater terrain, making it ideal for mapping and surveying purposes. On the other hand, Side Scan Sonar System produces detailed images of the seafloor and any objects on it by emitting sound waves in a side-scan pattern. Both types play crucial roles in underwater exploration, navigation, and research.</p></p>
