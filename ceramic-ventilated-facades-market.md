<p><p>Ceramic ventilated facades market types include Mechanical and Chemical Anchor Systems, Concealed Mechanical Anchor Systems, and Visible Mechanical Anchor Systems. The Mechanical and Chemical Anchor Systems use a combination of mechanical fasteners and chemical adhesives to secure the ceramic panels. Concealed Mechanical Anchor Systems hide the fasteners behind the panels for a seamless appearance. Visible Mechanical Anchor Systems have the fasteners exposed on the facade. Each system offers a different aesthetic and functional solution for securing ceramic ventilated facades.</p></p>
