<p><p>Accommodative intraocular lenses are used in cataract surgery to provide vision at various distances without the need for glasses. There are two main types of accommodative intraocular lenses: single optical surface and double optical surface. Single optical surface lenses have a single focal point, allowing for clear vision at one distance, typically either near or far. Double optical surface lenses have two focal points, enabling clear vision at both near and far distances, providing a wider range of vision correction options for patients.</p></p>
