Sample injectors are used to introduce samples into the system, while pumps are essential for controlling the flow rate of the solvent. Fraction collectors help in collecting and separating components of the sample. Detectors are crucial for analyzing the separated components. Other accessories may include columns, fittings, and software for data analysis. These accessories play a vital role in the efficient functioning of HPLC systems.</p></p>
