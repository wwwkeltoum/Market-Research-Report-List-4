<p><p>The industrial smoke alarm market consists of three main types: Photoelectric Smoke Alarms, Ionization Smoke Alarms, and Dual Sensor Smoke Alarms. Photoelectric alarms are designed to detect smoldering fires, while ionization alarms are more responsive to fast-flaming fires. Dual sensor alarms combine the features of both types for comprehensive fire detection. Each type of alarm is designed to provide early warning and help prevent potential industrial disasters by detecting smoke and fire in different ways.</p></p>
