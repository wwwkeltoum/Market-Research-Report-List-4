<p><p>Impact POS Receipt Printers use an ink ribbon to print on a paper roll, providing high-quality printed receipts with clear text and graphics. They are reliable and cost-effective, making them popular in various industries. On the other hand, Thermal POS Receipt Printers use heat to print on heat-sensitive paper, eliminating the need for ink ribbons and producing fast, quiet, and high-resolution prints. They are energy-efficient and low-maintenance, making them a preferred choice for many businesses.</p></p>
