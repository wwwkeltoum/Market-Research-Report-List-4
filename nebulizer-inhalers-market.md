<p><p>Nebulizer Inhalers market consists of three main types: Pneumatic Nebulizer Machines, Ultrasonic Nebulizer Machines, and Mesh Nebulizer Machines. Pneumatic nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency sound waves to create a fine mist. Mesh nebulizers use a vibrating mesh to generate medication particles for inhalation. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs and preferences of the patients.</p></p>
