<p><p>Dye Penetrant Testing is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials such as metals, plastics, ceramics, and composites. In this process, a colored dye is applied to the surface of the material, which then seeps into any cracks or flaws. After a specified dwell time, excess dye is removed and a developer is applied to draw out the trapped dye, making defects visible under UV light.</p><p>The Dye Penetrant Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period.
