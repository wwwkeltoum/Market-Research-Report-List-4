<p><p>Voice Recognition Biometrics market can be categorized into two types: Physiological Biometrics and Behavioral Biometrics. Physiological Biometrics involves analyzing physical characteristics of an individual's voice, such as vocal tract shape and size. On the other hand, Behavioral Biometrics focuses on patterns in an individual's speech, including intonation and accent. These two types of biometrics can be used in voice recognition technology to accurately authenticate and verify an individual's identity based on their unique voice characteristics.</p></p>
