<p><strong>The In-vitro Toxicity Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Well Plate System</li><li>Multi-compartmental Perfused Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-vitro toxicity testing market includes static well plate system and multi-compartmental perfused systems. The static well plate system involves growing cells in a single layer in wells of a culture plate, making it suitable for high-throughput screening of toxic substances. On the other hand, multi-compartmental perfused systems mimic the complex interactions between different tissues and organs in the human body, providing a more realistic representation of toxicity. Both types of testing methods are essential for evaluating the safety of chemicals and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
