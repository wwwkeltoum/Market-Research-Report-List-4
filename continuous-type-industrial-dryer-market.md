<p><p>Continuous type industrial dryers are used for large-scale drying applications in various industries. Tunnel dryers are long enclosed structures through which materials are continuously moved along a conveyor belt. Disc dryers consist of a series of heated discs that rotate and dry the material as it moves through them. Paddle dryers have heated paddles that mix and move the material, ensuring efficient and uniform drying. Each type of dryer offers unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the drying process.</p></p>
