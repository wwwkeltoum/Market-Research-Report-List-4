<p><p>The orthopedic implant material market can be segmented into four main types: metal, biological ceramic, polymer, and hard material. Polymer implants are lightweight and flexible, ideal for certain applications. Hard material implants, like carbon fiber or peek, offer high strength and wear-resistance. Each type of material has unique characteristics that cater to specific patient needs in orthopedic implant procedures.</p></p>
