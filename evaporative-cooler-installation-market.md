<p><strong>The Evaporative Cooler Installation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Downdraft</li><li>Sidedraft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Evaporative cooler installations can be categorized into two main market types: downdraft and sidedraft. Downdraft installations involve placing the cooler on the roof and allowing the cool air to be distributed downwards. This type is commonly used in residential settings. On the other hand, sidedraft installations involve placing the cooler on the side of a building, allowing the cool air to be distributed horizontally. This type is often used in commercial and industrial settings due to its efficient cooling capabilities.</p></p>
